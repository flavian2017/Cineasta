Privacy Policy for Cineasta

Effective Date: 31/12/2024

Thank you for using Cineasta! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect
Cineasta does not collect, store, or share any personal information or data. All information you provide, such as movies and ratings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

2. How We Use Your Information
Since no data is collected or shared, all movie and rating information you add is used solely within the app to maintain your personalized watchlist.

3. Data Storage
All data, including movies and ratings, is stored locally on your device. Cineasta does not require an internet connection, ensuring that your information remains private and secure.

4. Data Sharing
Cineasta does not share your data with anyone.

5. Security
As Cineasta does not access or store any data outside your device, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend enabling device-level security, such as passwords or biometric authentication, to further protect your data.

6. User Control
You have full control over your data. You can modify or delete your movie entries and ratings at any time directly through the app.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If changes occur, the updated Privacy Policy will be made available within the app, and the "Effective Date" will be revised.
